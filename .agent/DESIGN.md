# Design System: OpenClaw Agency
**Project ID:** 16297940785569805860

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design is **sophisticated, minimalist, and trustworthy**. It uses a high-contrast palette with generous whitespace to convey premium technical expertise. The "vibe" is focused and efficient, mirroring the core value of the service (saving time and money).

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Deep Obsidian (#1a1a1a)**: Used for primary headings, navigation links, and high-impact UI elements.
- **Electric Indigo (#4f46e5)**: The primary accent color, used for CTA buttons and critical interactive states.
- **Soft Alabaster (#f8fafc)**: The main background color, providing a clean and airy feel.
- **Muted Mist (#e2e8f0)**: Used for borders, dividers, and subtle background surfaces.
- **Steel Gray (#64748b)**: Used for body text to maintain optimal readability while being less harsh than pure black.

## 3. 2026 UI/UX Trends (Applied)
- **Liquid Glass**: Use adaptive transparency, frosted glass effects with `backdrop-blur-xl`, and specular highlights on obsidian elements.
- **Tactile Maximalism**: Implement "squishy" buttons with deep inner shadows, layered textures, and soft-lift cards that feel physically touchable.
- **3D & Spatial**: Utilize floating panels, parallax depth z-index stacks, and 3D icons/objects representing the OpenClaw "brain."
- **Agentic UX**: Design interface components that visualize "Reasoning Steps" or "Internal Workflows" of the AI agent, making the agency's work transparent and trustworthy.

## 4. Typography Rules
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Sans-serif).
- **Headings**: Semi-bold weight, increased letter-spacing for a modern feel.
- **Body**: Regular weight, optimal line-height (1.6) for readability.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons**: Pill-shaped or subtly rounded (8px), high contrast background (Indigo) with white text.
* **Cards/Containers**: Minimalist border (1px Mist) with no shadow or a very faint "whisper" shadow for light elevation.
* **Inputs/Forms**: Clean outlines, ample padding, focal focus states using the primary Indigo.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Whitespace**: Generous margins and padding between sections to maintain the minimalist aesthetic.
- **Grid**: Standard 12-column grid with a max-width container for desktop clarity.
- **Alignment**: Left-aligned headings with centered hero layouts for maximum impact.
